Noodles Recipe

Chow mein is a dish of Chinese stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu. Over the centuries, variations of chǎomiàn were developed in many regions of China; there are several methods of frying the noodles and a range of toppings can be used

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • carrots
  • green onions
  • cabbage
  • capsicum
  • green peas
  • tofu
  • soy sauce
  • seasame oil
  • vinegar
  • red chilli sauce
  • sugar
  • cornstarch

Steps of making noodles

  1. In a small mixing bowl, combine ingredients for chow mein sauce:sesame oil, soy sauce and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions, set aside.
  3. Heat a large wok or pan. Add a bit of oil to the pan and cook chicken until it’s golden brown.
  4. Add carrots, cabbage and pressed garlic and saute for a few minutes until veggies are slightly softened.
  5. followed by cooked noodles and pour the sauce right on top. Cook for about 2 minutes, distributing all that saucy goodness around evenly.
  6. Add chopped green onions and remove from the heat. Serve hot!
